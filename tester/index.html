Help Test Islamic Coloring for Kids! 🎨

Be one of the first to try our new app — and help us make it better before launch.

We've built a calm, ad-free Islamic coloring app for children, and we'd love your help testing it. It takes 2 minutes to join, and we just ask that you color a few pages over the next two weeks.

What you'll get to try

  • 58 coloring pages + a Free Draw canvas
  • Kaaba, mosques, nature, fruits, clothes, and all 28 Arabic letters
  • Kid Mode + Parental Gate for safe, worry-free use
  • No ads, no subscriptions

How to join (2 quick steps)

⚠️ Important: you're only counted as a tester once the app is installed and opened on your phone — please don't stop after step 1.

Easiest on your Android phone, start to finish, in one sitting — if you start on a computer and finish later, it's easy to forget step 2. On your phone you're already signed in to the right Google account; on a computer, sign in to the same account that's on your phone.

1. Join the tester group — the gate

This just lets Google know you're a tester. It's quick, but it doesn't count on its own.

Join the tester group →

2. Become a tester & install — this is the step that counts

Open the test link, tap "Become a tester", and install from Google Play. Then open the app and color one page — that's how your test registers.

Open the test link →

If you see a "no permission" or "sign in" message, sign in to your Google account first.

That's it!

Then please open the app and color a few pages over the next two weeks. If anything looks broken or confusing, tell us at support@kevroapps.com — your feedback shapes the final app.

Note: This is a test version, so you may spot the occasional rough edge — that's exactly what we want to hear about!
